Joon's FK8

Parts

In order to be considered FBO ('Full Bolt On'), you need to make sure you have since modded your FK8 to have an exhaust, front pipe, down pipe, turbo inlet, front mount intercooler, and an intake. I am currently only missing the inlet and FMIC, with plans to get both and buy a flex fuel kit.

Team

My crew or team is FK8Houston and we go to meets together as a team. Our goal is to have fun and express our love for our cars while showing we can represent the Type R scene in Houston properly.
